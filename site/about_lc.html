About Local Connect

Local Connect is Liberia's first community-powered e-commerce platform that aims to decentralised digital marketplace by connecting local sellers to buyers via app, offering real-time delivery coordination. We bridge the gap between Liberia's informal economy and digital growth

Who We Are

Local Connect is a community-powered platform that aims to help grow and enhance small businesses.

Our Mission

We aim to maximise local businesses' revenue/profit by connecting them to a broader range of customers as well as making shopping easy and convenient for buyers.

Our Vision

To boost the local economy by connecting local sellers to buyers and contributing to marketing their business in a way that's enable local sellers to reach a vast multitude of different customers as well as making online commercial activities very simple and convenient for buyers.

Core Values

Transparency and Trustworthiness
